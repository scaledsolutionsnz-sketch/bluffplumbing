What We Do

Complete Plumbing Solutions

From a dripping tap to a full new build, Justin and the team at Bluff Plumbing Services handle it all. Quality workmanship, every time.

General Plumbing and Repairs

Leaking pipes, blocked drains, dripping taps, toilet repairs and all general plumbing maintenance. Fast, reliable and done right the first time.

Hot Water Cylinders

Supply and installation of hot water cylinders, heat pump hot water systems, and repairs to all types of hot water heating. Keep the hot water flowing.

Gasfitting

Licensed gasfitter for all natural gas and LPG installations. Gas hobs, gas heaters, gas hot water, reticulation and compliance certificates.

Drainlaying

Registered drainlayer for all stormwater and wastewater drainage. New drain installations, blocked drain clearing, drain relining and repairs.

Bathroom and Kitchen Renovations

Full bathroom and kitchen plumbing for renovations. New showers, baths, vanities, toilets and full kitchen reconfigurations. We work with your builder or direct.

New Builds and Alterations

Plumbing fit-outs for new homes, extensions and alterations throughout Southland. From rough-in to commissioning, we handle every stage of the build.

Commercial Plumbing

Plumbing services for commercial properties, offices, shops and industrial sites. Maintenance contracts, fitouts and compliance work throughout Southland.

Leak Detection and Repairs

Suspect a hidden leak? We locate and repair water leaks under floors, inside walls and underground before they cause serious damage to your property.

About Justin

Your Local Licensed Plumber

Bluff Plumbing Services Ltd was founded by Justin Sutherland in 2017. Based right here in Bluff, Justin is a licensed plumber, gasfitter and registered drainlayer who takes pride in quality workmanship and looking after his community.

Whether it is a quick repair or a major renovation, Justin brings the same level of care and professionalism to every job. As a local himself, he understands what Southland homes and businesses need and he is always available to talk through the job before any work starts.
